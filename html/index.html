    About us

    About

    As we know, Japan is one of the developed and prosperous countries in the world. Its natural beauty, rich and diverse culture and life style have really fascinated people round the globe. Moreover, its technological breakthrough and educational advancement have created curiosity and attraction to the people all over the world. In this regard, Nepal and Nepalese cannot remain aloof from the influence of Japan. As a result, Japan has become a popular destination for abroad study among Nepalese Students and career seekers.
    Kl Japanese Language is an organization which aims to provide better learning experiences to those students who want to learn Japanese Language. KL Japanese Language started serving japanese language class to different students since 2014 A.D. If any students who is thinking to persue their education further in Japan, Kl Japanese Language institute is best for them.We succeed to exchange around 2000 students at Japan. Many of our students are currently studying in Japan in different colleges and universities. Some of them are also studying in different National universities of Japan.KL Japanese Language School has been one of the best institute in eastern part of Kathmandu valley. It was established in 2014 A.D. initially as KL Japanese Language School and has been a tremendous institute for spreading quality education. It has been basically running in three different time i.e it has been running morning shift, day shift, evening shift. It has carved for itself a brilliant educational trajectory since its inception.
    We are located at Putalisadak, Kathmandu. We warmly welcome you all and promise to provide best services to you.


    OUR MISSION
    To established itself as a world class learning center to inculcating positive attitude, skills, ability and value based knowledge in students to compete successful in their chosen discipline and helps to build their career.

    OUR AIM
    The main aim of the institution is to make students Learn to write, read and speak the language with the Complete Japanese course of offered by the institute and helping the students to have a better future in Japan.
